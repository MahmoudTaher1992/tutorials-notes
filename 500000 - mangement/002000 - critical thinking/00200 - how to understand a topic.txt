How to understand a topic
    > try to write down the output as you follow the steps

    > Identify the main topic concepts
        > List down the concepts
        > List down the keywords
        > List down the goals

    > Material gathering
        > i.e.
            > books
            > websites
            > best authors
            > videos
            > files

    > set a time box for the topic research

    > Organize the materials
        > into
            > categories
            > subtopics

    > update the main topic concepts as you dive into the materials

    > Analyze the information
        > Evaluate the information
        > Identify the connection between different pieces of information
        > Identify patterns
        > Take notes 
        > Summarize the information
        > create mind maps
