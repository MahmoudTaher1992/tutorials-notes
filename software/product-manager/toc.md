## Part II: The Product Development Lifecycle

### A. Understanding the Lifecycle Stages
- **Introduction/Ideation:** Finding a problem worth solving.
- **Growth:** Finding product-market fit and scaling the user base.
- **Maturity:** Optimizing, defending market share, and extending value.
- **Decline:** Sunsetting, pivoting, or replacing the product.
- The PM's role and focus in each stage.

### B. Ideation & Opportunity Discovery
- Sources of Ideas (User Feedback, Internal Teams, Market Trends, Data)

  - TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving)
- **Problem Framing**
  - The "5 Whys" Technique
  - Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) Framework
  - Problem Statements & Opportunity Canvases

## Part VII: Metrics, Data & Growth
