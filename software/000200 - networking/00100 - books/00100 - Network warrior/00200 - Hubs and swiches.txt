Hub and switches
    > hub
        > multi port device
        > dumb device
        > sends data to all ports
        > if computer sends data to another computer, hub sends the data to all ports

        > types
            > active
                > has a power supply that strength the signal if it is received weak
            > passive
                > does not have a power supply

    > switch
        > device used to connect devices together
        > receives the data and sends it to the correct port, not all ports
        > 

    > Router
        > Network appliance device
        > moves the data packets from one network to another
