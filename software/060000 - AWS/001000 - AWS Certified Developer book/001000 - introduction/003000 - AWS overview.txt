AWS overview
    > AWS regions and availability zones
        > AWS regions
            > Separate geographic area
            > Has multiple isolated locations called availability zones
            > Each region is independent and isolated to keep them safe from catastrophic events
            > AZs in each region is connected using low latency networks

        > It is recommended to choose the nearest region to your customers to minimize latency
        > If the region has a problem with the services you provide then you will have to choose another region

    > SaaS, PaaS, IaaS
        > IaaS
            > Service providers offers virtualized hardware or computing
            > i.e (digitalocean)

        > PaaS
            > Service providers offers software platform
            > You don't have to deal w. hardware or main platform

        > SaaS
            > Service providers offers software on top of software platform
