Vector databases
    > is a type of database specifically designed to store, manage, and search vector embeddings efficiently
        > focus on storing data as vectors not as rows and columns
    > concepts
        > vector
            > numerical representation of a variable
        > Similarity Search
            > finding the vectors that are most similar to a given query vector

    > vector databases benefits
        > Efficient Similarity Search
        > Semantic Search
            > search the data based on the meaning not just the keywords
