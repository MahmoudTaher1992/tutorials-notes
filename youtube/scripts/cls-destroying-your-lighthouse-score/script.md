# CLS: The Web Vital Hurting Your Score

**Tone:** Serious / Educational
**Duration:** ~30 seconds

| Sentence | Type |
|----------|------|
| Your users are mis-clicking buttons because your layout is jumping around. | Hook |

| The most common cause: images without explicit width and height attributes. | Body – Point 1 |

| Fix it by adding `width` and `height` to every `<img>` tag. | Body – Point 3 |
