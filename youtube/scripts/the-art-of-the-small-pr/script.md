# Why Nobody Reviews Your Giant PRs

**Tone:** Serious / Educational
**Duration:** ~30 seconds

| Sentence | Type |
|----------|------|
| Nobody is genuinely reviewing your 1200-line pull request. | Hook |
| Here is why small PRs get better reviews, faster. | Title Reveal |
| Large diffs trigger cognitive overload — reviewers skim instead of reviewing. | Introduction |
| PRs over 400 lines see a steep drop in review thoroughness. | Body – Point 1 |
| One PR, one concern — two problems means two PRs. | Body – Point 2 |
| Split by layer: data model first, then API endpoint, then UI. | Body – Point 3 |
| Small PRs catch more bugs, merge faster, and are easier to revert. | Conclusion |
| Keep your next PR under 200 lines — your reviewers will thank you. | CTA |
