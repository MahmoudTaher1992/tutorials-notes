EC2 Instance Store
    > A high performance hardware disk 
        > physical disk connected to a physical machine that hosts your EC2 instance
    > Differs from EBS volumes in
        > EC2 Instance Store has a higher performance 
        > EC2 Instance Store loses their storage if they are stopped
    > Good for
        > buffer
        > cache
        > scratch data
        > temporary content
    > Risk data loss if hardware fails
    > Backups and replication are your responsibility
